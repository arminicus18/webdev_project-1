Hiker's Guide

Essential tips for a safe and responsible adventure.

"Take nothing but pictures, leave nothing but footprints."

  • Dispose of Waste Properly: Apply the "Basura Mo, Iuwi Mo" policy.
  • Respect Wildlife: Keep a safe distance. Do not feed animals.
  • Be Considerate: Keep noise levels down. Nature is not a disco.

0% Ready

Mountaineering is physically demanding. Be honest with your guide.

  • Medical Declaration: Declare asthma, hypertension, or heart issues.
  • Cut-off Times: Respect mandatory descent times.
  • Physical Prep: Jogging/Cardio 1 week prior is required.
